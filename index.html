Iman Hejazi

Student of Metallurgy and Welding Engineering

Welcome to my academic homepage at Github. I am MSc student of Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT), one of the most prestigious universities and the first established technical university in Iran at the Department of Mining and Metallurgy. I have BSc degree in Technical Inspection Engineering from Petroleum University of Technology (PUT), Iran.

My specific field of interest is High-Tech. and new born welding methods specially Solid State Friction Stir Welding (FSW) and currently, I am working on two parallel projects in this field. Double-Sided FSW and T-Joint FSW welds are major of my interests.

I also have ~10 years programming experience in different languages such as PHP, ASP, MySQL, C#, JavaScript &... . I'm a Professional Web Designer and Developer. It's my hobbie!

I'm so enthusiastic to continue my education toward the highest levels in science and expand my knowledge about Materials Engineering and world problems as a researcher and improve my intellectual abilities so as to satisfy my desire toward learning.
